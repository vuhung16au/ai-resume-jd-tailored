The Billing and Credit function is part of the Group Finance and Corporate division which is responsible for the overall management of Billing and Credit Management across Optus. This includes credit and fraud risk assessment, collections and recoveries, and billing and payments. As part of this high performing Billing and Credit team, you will live their vision - We onboard and retain our customers efficiently whilst delivering timely, simple, accurate billing and payments experience.

Your day in the life of a Senior Data Scientist at Optus

Lead delivery of all advanced scientific data models and analytics across Optus Group, supporting both Credit decisions and Fraud risk management, including the development and maintenance of credit scorecards, policy rule updates, payment fraud detection, device intelligence, network fraud analytics, and fraud model improvements.
Own the design and strategy/policy changes responsible for managing over 3 million applications per year through automated systems decisions within SLA.
Plan and supervise the key priorities for Group Credit and Finance to meet annual goals and business targets.
Partner with all relevant senior stakeholders to deliver improved financial performance across Optus Group.
Translate complex analysis into easily understood concepts and reporting for business partners.
Why you are our next professional

Experience in a Finance/Data Science role with a specialisation in data modelling and strong capability in performing data processing tasks (auditing, preparation, transformation, aggregation) using Databricks, SQL, Python or R.
3-7 years proven experience in developing credit risk evaluation models or fraud prevention/detection models for customer originations, with background in payment fraud, device intelligence data, and Collections considered highly desirable.
Proven ability to lead the development and deployment of machine learning models, ensuring ongoing alignment with business requirements, using supervised/unsupervised techniques and libraries such as scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and XGBoost.
Experience with model risk management frameworks, including real-world fraud detection applications across the customer origination journey.
Ability to work in an environment of constant change and diverse challenges within a highly complicated market, backed by strong analytical problem-solving, communication and presentation skills.
BA/BS degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, Analytics, or an equivalent applied quantitative field.
